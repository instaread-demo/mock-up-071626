Razer, in partnership with The Pokémon Company International, has officially launched the Razer | Espeon & Umbreon Collection. Released to coincide with Pokémon’s 30th anniversary, the new lineup features peripheral designs inspired by the contrasting traits of the popular Eevee evolutions: the psychic-type Espeon and the dark-type Umbreon.

The collection integrates custom Pokémon aesthetics with standard Razer features, including ergonomic form factors, responsive hardware components, and customizable Razer Chroma RGB lighting. The themed collection consists of four distinct gaming peripherals designed for day-and-night setups.

Included Peripherals and Specifications

  • Razer Kraken V4 X – Espeon & Umbreon Edition: A wired gaming headset featuring custom earcup cushions, integrated Chroma RGB illumination, and high-clarity audio drivers.
  • Razer DeathAdder V3 HyperSpeed – Espeon & Umbreon Edition: A wireless gaming mouse with Focus Pro 35K optical sensor, up to 100 hours of battery life, and themed finish with Chroma RGB underglow.
  • Razer BlackWidow V4 75% – Espeon & Umbreon Edition: A compact mechanical keyboard with hot-swappable Razer Orange Tactile switches, custom Pokémon keycaps, and per-key Chroma RGB lighting.
  • Razer Goliathus Extended Chroma – Espeon & Umbreon Edition: A soft gaming mouse mat featuring a full-surface Espeon and Umbreon design with Chroma RGB edge lighting.

Each peripheral in the collection features dual-themed artwork representing Espeon’s psychic energy patterns and Umbreon’s dark-type ring markings. The Chroma RGB integration allows users to cycle between color profiles matching either character.

The Razer | Espeon & Umbreon Collection is available now through Razer.com and select retail partners. Pricing varies by product, with the full collection designed as a coordinated gaming setup.
