Where to find elevated cocktails in Des Moines

The frozen supper club cocktail is officially back in style. Across the Des Moines metro, restaurants are reviving the creamy, indulgent dessert drinks that once defined Midwestern nights out — the Grasshoppers your parents ordered after steak dinners, the Brandy Alexanders that capped off anniversaries, the Pink Squirrels that felt like a celebration in a glass.

Des Moines is rediscovering the joy of the frozen supper club drink. Johnny’s Italian Steakhouse brings the polished, Rat Pack-era version, Jesse’s Embers keeps the old school flame burning and Zombie Burger offers the wild, spiked shake interpretation.

It’s a summer built for creamy cocktails. So raise a glass. Cool off. Toast to a metro that knows how to have fun.

Johnny’s Italian Steakhouse

Johnny’s Italian Steakhouse brings boozy ice cream cocktails of yore back to the menu. Look for Golden Cadillacs, Grasshoppers, Pink Squirrels and Brandy Alexanders. Provided By Johnny’s Italian Steakhouse

This summer, the revival gets its biggest spotlight yet: Johnny’s Italian Steakhouse has launched a full menu of boozy ice cream cocktails, a nostalgic, Rat Pack-era throwback designed to make dessert feel like an experience again.
