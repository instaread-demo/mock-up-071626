Tommy Robinson has apologised to a Scottish dad who was falsely accused of filming kids in a park by anti-immigration protestors.

Quroum, 43, from Glasgow, had been playing with his children at Richmond Park in the Gorbals area of the city when a protest began to approach at around 9pm on Sunday, July 12.

Article continues below

The dad-of-three got into a confrontation with protestors, but was later targeted by online trolls after a video of his stand-off was shared on X by Tommy Robinson who branded him an “invader”.

Article continues below

The footage has now been deleted by Robinson, who today posted on X: “I have to correct the record here. In this instance I got it wrong, as did many others, so for that I apologise and I have deleted that post.”
