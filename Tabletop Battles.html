After a hefty delay thanks to unexpected warp turbulence (the best efforts of the postal service), Rich and Blake have got the new Ciaphas Cain miniature in hand. Our thanks to Games Workshop for providing advanced preview copies of this kit.

What’s In The Box

Why it’s Ciaphas Cain, the Hero of the Imperium! This limited-release set contains plastic miniatures for Cain and his long-suffering sidekick, Ferik Jurgen, mounted on 25mm bases or, optionally, a 40mm diorama base (more on this later).

Ciaphas Cain & Jurgen. Credit: Games Workshop/Warhammer Community

It feels like almost every new Warhammer kit these days is a candidate for “the sharpest detail I’ve ever seen” and this is true again here. Neither of these are complex models in terms of posing or equipment, but the faces in particular are incredibly finely detailed, to the point that I imagine hobbyists will need to be careful with their primer in order to avoid erasing some of it. Those wizards at the Citadel Miniatures factory keep on working their magic.

Ciaphas Cain sprue/box contents. Credit: Rich Nutter

Of note to fans of the Ciaphas Cain novels, I think it’s fair to say that this depiction of the illustrious Commissar is slightly more down to earth than some of the more intentionally propaganda-styled pieces of Black Library artwork. There are no torn uniforms stretched over impossibly large pectorals here, with Cain instead depicted as solid, rugged, arguably handsome from certain angles, but most definitely the size and shape of a normal man.
