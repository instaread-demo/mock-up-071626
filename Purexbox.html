ASUS Issues Mini Statement Confirming Standalone Version Of ROG Xbox Ally X20

It won’t just be paired with the AR glasses

When ASUS first revealed the ROG Xbox Ally X20 back at the start of June, it was only mentioned to be releasing as part of a bundle with the ROG XREAL R1 Edition 20 Gaming AR Glasses, but a new statement from the company has now confirmed that it’ll be sold standalone as well (as we always expected to be honest!).

This is coming from The Verge, which was contacted by an ASUS spokesperson to reveal that the company is working on the release schedule for this standalone version, although we don’t have a date or price as of yet.

Asus will sell a standalone version of the OLED Ally as well, spokesperson Anthony Spence confirms to The Verge. “We are actively discussing the release schedule for a standalone version of the new Ally. Please stay tuned for upcoming announcements.”

If you don’t recall, the main difference between the ROG Xbox Ally X and the ROG Xbox Ally X20 is the bigger OLED screen, although it also features other improvements such as TMR joysticks, a new transforming d-pad, redesigned face buttons and rubberised grips for extra comfort.
