Ex-Ram Ben Osbourne, along with his long-term pal Harry Steare, opened the doors to El Camino in Victoria Avenue, Borrowash, back in January following the success of the pair’s first venture into hospitality at the Lonely Boy Bar a few doors down.

It acts as a cafe on Mondays and Wednesdays (it’s closed on Tuesdays), offering high-quality coffee, cakes and snacks, before focusing on small plates and Mediterranean-inspired specials from Wednesday evening to Saturday.
